About us

Java & Coffee is a team of Trinity College students dedicated to locating good coffee and a good atmosphere near the college campus.

We’ve created this website to showcase our favourite Café’s we’ve discovered around Dublin.

We have a passion for coffee and wanted to share our love with others.

We’ve compiled a list of coffee shops based on their price, the quality of coffee, the ambience and the location.

We hope you enjoy our recommendations!
